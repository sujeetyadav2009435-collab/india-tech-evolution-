80 Years of Breakthroughs

1963
ISRO founded (INCOSPAR) — Thumba Equatorial Rocket Launching Station
1969
Tarapur Atomic Power Station — India’s first nuclear power plant
1980
SLV-3 success — First indigenous satellite launch vehicle
2008
Chandrayaan-1 — First lunar probe, discovers water molecules
2014
Mangalyaan (MOM) — India becomes 1st nation to succeed Mars orbit in first attempt
2016
UPI launched by NPCI — Digital payments revolution begins
2022
National Green Hydrogen Mission launched
2023
Chandrayaan-3 — Vikram lands successfully on Moon’s south pole
2024–25
Semiconductor fabs approved + 110 GW Solar milestone
2026
Gaganyaan crewed mission target • 500 GW non-fossil power target on track
